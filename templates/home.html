How PredictCart Works

Three-stage processing pipeline powered by PyTorch

01

Input Processing

Product name encoded via BERT (768-dim), category one-hot encoded, and numeric features normalized.

02

Transformer Encoding

Multi-head attention (4 heads) processes combined embeddings through 2 transformer layers with dropout regularization.

03

Price Regression

Attention-pooled representations fed through MLP head to predict log-price with confidence estimation.
