Our Mission

PredictCart was built to democratize AI-powered pricing intelligence for e-commerce businesses of all sizes. We believe that advanced machine learning shouldn't be reserved for tech giants — every merchant deserves data-driven pricing insights.

The Technology

Our multimodal transformer architecture represents the cutting edge of price prediction technology. By combining BERT's natural language understanding with category intelligence and market signals, we achieve industry-leading accuracy of 85%+.

The model was trained on over 100,000 real Amazon product listings across 19 categories, learning complex pricing patterns that traditional methods miss. From luxury electronics to everyday essentials, PredictCart understands the nuances that determine market value.

Project Background

Developed as a research project exploring multimodal machine learning applications in e-commerce, PredictCart demonstrates how transformer architectures can be adapted beyond NLP to solve real-world business problems.

The project showcases modern MLOps practices including model versioning, feature engineering pipelines, and production deployment strategies. It's designed to be both a practical tool and a learning resource for developers interested in applied machine learning.
