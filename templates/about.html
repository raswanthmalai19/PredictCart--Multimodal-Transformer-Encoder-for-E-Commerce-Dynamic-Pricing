Our Mission

PredictCart was built to predict realistic prices for Amazon India products in the budget to mid-range segment (₹200–₹25,000). Using a multimodal transformer architecture, it combines product name understanding, category intelligence, and market signals like ratings and discounts to give you an instant price estimate.

The Technology

Our model combines BERT's natural language understanding with category encoding and numeric features (ratings, reviews, discount ratio) using a compact Transformer (d_model=128, 4 attention heads, 2 layers, ~340K parameters). It was trained on real Amazon India product listings across categories like accessories, camera gear, sports equipment, home & kitchen, baby products, and books.

The model predicts in log-price space and is most accurate for products priced ₹200–₹25,000. It is not designed for premium smartphones, high-end laptops, or luxury items. Think of it as a smart pricing assistant for everyday Amazon India product categories.

Project Background

Developed as a research and learning project exploring multimodal machine learning for Amazon India e-commerce pricing, PredictCart demonstrates how BERT + Transformer architectures can be applied to real-world structured prediction problems.

The project is intentionally scoped to the budget/mid-range Amazon India product space (₹200–₹25,000) where the training data is most dense. The model has been validated on products like camera accessories, bags & luggage, sports gear, home appliances, and baby products — the kinds of items that dominate Amazon India's catalog volume.
